Shimla Manali Tour
5 Nights 6 Days
Monuments
Tour Itinerary

Delhi - Shimla

On Arrival at Delhi Airport / Railway Station, an Jingo Holidays representative offers an assisted transfer to Shimla, Fondly referred to as the ‘Queen of Hills’, Shimla is a very popular hill town of Himachal Pradesh. As you reach, check-in at the hotel in the evening free to visit Mall road. Overnight stay in hotel.

Day 02

Shimla

After breakfast proceed to full day sightseeing of Jakhoo Temple, Chirst Church, Hanuman Temple, Evening free to enjoy the scenic beauty of Kufri, overnight stay in hotel.

 

Day 03

Shimla - Manali

After breakfast Drive to Manali enroute Visit Kullu Manikaran, In evening Check in the hotel, overnight stay in hotel.

Day 04 

Manali Local Sightseeing

Post Breakfast proceed to full day sightseeing Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, Vashisht Temple and Tibetan Monastery, Club House, the vashisht hot water, Evening free to visit mall road, overnight stay in hotel

Day 05

Manali - Rohtang Pass/ Solang Velley

Post Breakfast proceed to Full day sightseeing of Solang Valley (Snow Point), overnight Stay In Hotel.

Day 06

Manali - Delhi

Morning check out from hotel, proceed to Delhi, Drop at Delhi airport, railway station with onward journey
